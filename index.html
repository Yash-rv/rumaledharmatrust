Welcome to Rumale Dharma Trust

Empowering communities, enriching lives, and spreading hope. Join us in making a difference.

Rumale Dharma Trust is a nonprofit organization with a heartfelt mission to support underprivileged families and foster a sense of community. The Trust believes that everyone, regardless of financial circumstances, deserves the opportunity to celebrate life's precious moments in a dignified way.

Our Flagship Initiative

One of our flagship initiatives is the funding of a spacious party hall, designed specifically for those who cannot afford expensive venues. This space is available to families for hosting joyous occasions such as weddings, birthday parties, anniversaries, or other milestone celebrations. By eliminating the financial burden of venue costs, the Trust helps create memories that families can cherish forever.

Our Vision

Rumale Dharma Trust is committed to uplifting lives and spreading happiness by ensuring that every family has a space to celebrate their joys and connect with their community, regardless of their economic background.

Our Mission

At Rumale Dharma Trust, our mission is to empower communities by providing accessible spaces for celebrations, education, and creativity. We aim to bridge economic divides, fostering unity and inclusivity through initiatives that bring people together and encourage mutual support.

Our Services

Joyous Family Celebrations

Host memorable occasions like weddings, birthday parties, anniversaries, or other milestone events. The party hall ensures these precious moments are celebrated in style without financial stress.

Community Activities

The party hall transforms into a vibrant venue for exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events, bringing the community together to celebrate art, education, and local initiatives.

A Platform for Local Talent

This multipurpose space fosters creativity and community spirit by offering a stage for talent to shine. It’s more than a venue—it's a place where ideas and dreams come alive.
